02 — Analitički okvir / Framework

Četveroslojni analitički okvir

Four-layer analytical framework — the corpus is read from raw data upward to interpreted discourse, each layer feeding the next.

01

Korpus i podaci

Corpus & Data

Collection, de-duplication, language identification and cleaning of 610,000 posts from portals, social platforms and forums.

ingest dedupe lang-id
02

Akteri

Actors

Who publishes — accounts and outlets are classified into four actor archetypes that shape how themes circulate.

classify archetypes
03

Teme

Themes

Content is assigned to 16 thematic categories through supervised classification and topic modeling.

supervised topic-model
04

Diskurs

Discourse

Framing, sentiment and network structure are analyzed and interpreted to describe how Catholic discourse is constructed online.

framing sentiment networks
03 — Akteri / Actor archetypes

Četiri arhetipa aktera

Four actor archetypes — a reusable card pattern for the agents that produce and relay religious content.

Institucija

Institution

Official Church bodies, dioceses and Catholic outlets publishing in their own name.

~12% objava

Posrednik

Amplifier

Secular media and aggregators that relay, reframe and amplify religious topics.

~34% objava

Kritičar

Critic

Oppositional and secular voices contesting religious claims and institutions.

~19% objava

Sudionik

Participant

Lay individuals — commenters and forum members discussing faith in everyday terms.

~35% objava

05 — Studije / Thematic studies

Pet tematskih studija

Five thematic studies — the project's published and ongoing outputs, each drawing on the shared corpus and framework.

S1

Mapiranje medijskog prostora

Mapping the mediascape

A descriptive baseline of the full corpus: volume, sources and thematic distribution over five years.

descriptive corpus ● Objavljeno
S2

Polarizacija oko bioetike

Polarization around bioethics

How life and bioethics debates split actors, and where the sharpest discursive divides appear.

sentiment networks ● U tijeku
S3

Crkva i pandemija

The Church during the pandemic

Religious discourse under COVID-19 — restrictions, trust and the shift of worship online.

time-series topics ● Objavljeno
S4

Povjerenje, kler i skandali

Trust, clergy and scandal
