# /research-ideation — sharpen a research question (DigiKat-calibrated)

## Instructions
1. **Load** `.claude/references/discipline-card-digikat.md` (descriptive tilt; population–context–mechanism
   framing; APA/Chicago).
2. **Delegate** to `scholar-skill:scholar-idea` to formalize the question (population / context / mechanism),
   and to `scholar-skill:scholar-hypothesis` ONLY if the study is genuinely confirmatory.
3. **DigiKat fit check.** Can the corpus actually answer it (platforms covered, 2021–2025, the ≥2-term
   inclusion filter, sample size)? Name the corpus slice it would use.
4. **Preregistration note.** Most DigiKat studies are descriptive → no prereg. Flag prereg ONLY if a
   confirmatory directional hypothesis on a not-yet-examined slice emerges (e.g. Study 3, christian-democracy).
5. **Output** the refined RQ (+ optional hypotheses), the data slice, and the next step (`/lit-review` or `/new-study`).
